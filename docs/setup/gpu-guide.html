🎮 Setup — GPU Guide

GPU Recommendations 2026

Which GPU to buy or rent for SLM development. Sorted by value, not marketing.

⏱ 8 min read

Quick Recommendation

💚
Start free: Colab T4
Google Colab T4 16GB is free. Fine-tunes 1B models. Zero cost to validate your project.
💙
Best value: RTX 3090 (owned)
24GB VRAM. ~$500 used. Fine-tunes all models up to 7B with QLoRA. Unlimited hours.
🧡
Best cloud: RunPod RTX 3090
$0.20/hr. Pay only when training. Better than owning until you exceed ~2,500 hours/year.
💜
Best performance: RTX 4090
24GB VRAM, 2x faster than 3090. ~$1,600 new. For builders who train daily.

Rule of thumb

For QLoRA, you need roughly 6–8 GB VRAM per billion parameters at 4-bit. A 1B model needs 6 GB, a 7B needs ~16 GB, a 13B needs ~32 GB.
